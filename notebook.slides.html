Making an optimal marketing strategy for our insurance¶

We have been provided plenty of data from our customers and non customers from our agency.

We will try using those data to find a way to plan a good marketing strategy to improve sales.

The main question we were asked is :

Are our customers more likely to travel abroad and travel more often compare to non customers ?

The data we were provided can help us make additional supposition to have a better insite on potential customers.

Conclusion¶

We have answered a couple of question here :

Are our customers more likely to travel abroad and travel more often compare to non customers ?

Yes, it is seems the first assumption made here seems to be true.µ

Does the frenquency of flying is affected by annual income ? What about it when we consider customers ? Yes, Totally it seems the higher the income, the higher the chance to fly a lot and buy an insurance.

Does having more family members make you more likely to buy a travel insurance ?

Not really, the difference of the numbers of family members isn't different between client or not.

Does age affect how likely you are to buy a travel insurance ?

No, the data give us that there is not difference because of how old you are ?

Which sector are more likely to travel and buy insurance ?

Public sector are more likely to buy insurance and the percentage of Frequent Flyers in this group is a bit bigger!
